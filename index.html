Susung Hong

Welcome to my website! I am a Ph.D. student at the Allen School at the University of Washington, co-advised by Ira Kemelmacher-Shlizerman, Steve Seitz, and Brian Curless.

My research interests lie in diffusion models for image/video/3D/4D generation and their potential to understand and construct sophisticated environments.
